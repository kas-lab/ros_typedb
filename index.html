Welcome to ros_typedb’s documentation!

ros_typedb provides a basic generic integration between ROS and typeDB. It was designed in a way to enable users to easily extend it to fulfill their needs, its design is explained in the Design section.

This package was tested in Ubuntu 22.04 with ROS Humble and typedb v2.24.17.

ros_typedb is in an initial stage and under constant development
